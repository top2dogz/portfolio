About Me

My name is Izaac Salvador Ruiz, and welcome to my website! I am currently a third-year student @ UC Berkeley, studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences and I have decided to create a website for myself for three main reasons.

  • For one, I've come to the conclusion that I finally need some sort of platform to show what I've done at school. Besides the professional advantages of owning my own domain on the internet and all that stuff, I really just want a simple and concise way to display all my accomplishments to friends and family.
  • I've always wanted to create a website for myself. In terms of goals, building a website has always been a close second on the list, right under the minecraft server that I started a few summers ago.
  • 3, It's pretty much the most convenient option in terms of showcasing everything. To be brief, it's much more convenient to tell my parents to visit a website rather than having tirelessly explain GitHub to them

    • At the time of creation, I intend for this website to mainly function as an interactive portfolio, but who knows what this might turn out to be. For now, enjoy my website.
